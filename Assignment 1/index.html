About the song

'Nowhere Man' is a song about someone with a directionless life, as well as no real worldview. It was one of the first Beatles songs to not be about romance or love. In 1968, the song was featured in the Beatles animated film 'Yellow Submarine', where the group sings the song upon meeting the nowhere man himself in Nowhere Land.
